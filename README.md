# KalyxX Water Technology Landing Page

## Overview

This landing page provides a comprehensive, conversion-optimized structure for Swiss Aqua Technologies KalyxX water improvement products. The page showcases the full range of IPS (Ion Polarization System) water treatment solutions including residential, commercial, industrial, and specialized applications.

## Product Family

### Residential Systems
- **KalyxX Mini** - Home filter with IPS100 technology, 75μm accuracy, 0,9 m³/h flow rate
- **KalyxX Active** - Advanced residential system with antibacterial protection, 90% limescale reduction

### Commercial Systems
- **KalyxX BlueLine** - Premium commercial solution, 76% limescale reduction with antibacterial benefits
- **KalyxX RedLine** - Heavy-duty industrial solution, 76% limescale reduction

### Specialized Systems
- **KalyxX GreenLine** - Agricultural irrigation system, 76% limescale reduction for farms
- **WashBall** - Laundry water treatment system, point-of-use solution
- **KalyxX Industry** - Municipal and large-scale industrial systems, up to 100 m³/h

## Technical Specifications

### Core Technology - IPS (Ion Polarization System)
- **TGP® Technology**: Turbulent Galvanic Polarization
- **No chemicals required**
- **No external energy needed**
- **Environmentally friendly**
- **Lifespan**: 10+ years with proper maintenance

### Key Specifications

#### KalyxX Mini
- Filter accuracy: 75 μm
- Water flow rate: 0,9 m³/h
- Working pressure: 0,75 MPa
- Temperature: 1 °C - 50 °C
- Max hardness: < 56 °dH (10 mmol/l)
- Service life: 12 months or 22,000 liters
- Warranty: 2 years

#### KalyxX Active
- Water flow rate: Up to 4 m³/h
- Working pressure: 10 bar
- Temperature: 0 °C to 85 °C
- Efficiency: 90% limescale reduction
- Antibacterial: Yes (silver electrodes)
- NRL certified for drinking water contact
- Warranty: 2 years

#### KalyxX BlueLine
- Water flow rate: Up to 7 m³/h
- Working pressure: 10 bar
- Temperature: 0 °C to 85 °C
- Efficiency: 76% limescale reduction
- Antibacterial: Yes (40% reduction)
- NRL certified for drinking water contact
- Warranty: 2 years

#### KalyxX RedLine
- Water flow rate: Up to 7 m³/h
- Working pressure: 10 bar
- Temperature: 0 °C to 85 °C
- Efficiency: 76% limescale reduction
- NRL certified for drinking water contact
- Warranty: 2 years

#### KalyxX GreenLine
- Water flow rate: Up to 4 m³/h
- Working pressure: 10 bar
- Temperature: 0 °C to 35 °C
- Efficiency: Up to 76% limescale reduction
- NRL certified for drinking water contact
- Specialized for agricultural irrigation
- Warranty: 2 years

#### KalyxX Industry
- Water flow rate: Up to 100 m³/h
- Working pressure: Up to 12 bar
- Temperature: 0 °C to 35 °C
- Efficiency: 90% (Active) / 76% (BlueLine/RedLine)
- Connection: DN40-DN125 (flange) or DN40-DN150
- Specialized for municipalities, factories, restaurants
- Warranty: 2 years

#### WashBall
- Water flow rate: 2-5 GPM
- No electricity required
- Point-of-use laundry treatment
- Changes calcite to aragonite
- Reduces detergent consumption

How the IPS WashBall works

IPS WashBall effectively changes hard crystals of calcite to aragonite

IPS WashBall changes hard crystals of calcite to aragonite. This causes the water to act like softer, which as a result increases washing efficiency of all detergents and protects your washing machine from limescale. Our and also independent tests prove that IPS WashBall significantly improves washing results.
